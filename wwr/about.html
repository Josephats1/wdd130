History

Over the years, we've grown to become one of the region's most trusted rafting companies, with thousands of satisfied customers and a perfect safety record.

Founded in 2005 by outdoor enthusiasts, RiverRush Adventures began with a single raft and a passion for sharing the beauty of river rapids.
